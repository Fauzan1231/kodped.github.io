What is roleplay and how to play it
OK, let's tell you this

Kotapedia Roleplay

GTA 5 Roleplay is a modification that allows players to explore the in-game world of GTA 5 in a different way. It's not just about completing the missions of the core game, but rather creating an immersive role-playing experience. Each player creates their own unique character and chooses the role they want to play, from being a police officer who maintains order, to a paramedic who saves lives, to a criminal who plans his crimes.

PEMERINTAH KOTAPEDIA

What's the PEMERINTAH KOTAPEDIA job to do? It's to guide new citizens who are new to playing roleplay so they understand what roleplay is and how to play it, and not only that, it's the PEMERINTAH KOTAPEDIA job, they can also receive goods for sale at the Manpower Department and also mingle with the citizens.

POLPEDIA

What is POLPEDIA's job? So POLPEDIA's job is to regulate and monitor citizens and keep the city safe from crime, such as robbing shops, small banks, big banks, etc., because in this roleplay many people act out their characters as gangsters and there are many more tasks.

KOTAPEDIA MEDICAL

What are KOTAPEDIA MEDICAL's duties doing? Emergency Medical Services: Providing first aid and medical treatment to injured or sick characters in the game. Medical Training: Providing training to other players regarding first aid measures or basic medical procedures.
